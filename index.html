Why Patients Choose Us

A Small Clinic That Runs
On Time and Takes Its Time

We deliberately stay small. I'm the only dentist here — not a corporate chain with rotating doctors who don't know your case history. You see me every visit.

I started practicing in 2010 at a chain clinic and saw how volume-based dentistry leads to overtreatment. I opened my own clinic in Koramangala in 2015. Most patients who come here have been to three other dentists before me. They're tired of being told they need work they don't actually need.

No Overtreatment

I've turned down cosmetic cases where a cleaning was all the patient needed. I won't push veneers on you. If your teeth are healthy, I'll tell you.

Sterilization Protocol

Every instrument is autoclaved. Our sterilization protocol was developed during COVID and we've kept it because it was always the right standard.

Predictable Pricing

Every treatment plan includes a full itemized cost breakdown before we start. No billing surprises.

For Anxious Patients

We take extra time with patients who've had bad experiences. The first visit is usually just talking — no work, no pressure.
